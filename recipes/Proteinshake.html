Protein shake recipe

Ingredients

  • Vanilla Whey Protein Powder one and a half scoops
  • One Cup Of Milk
  • One half cup of Non-Fat Greek Yogurt
  • One Scoop of Peanut Butter
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  1. put the protein powder and the milk in a small bowl and start to wisk these two until they are liquid
  2. Add one cup of strawberries and blueberries to the Ninja Blender
  3. Add the Greek yogurt and the scoop of Peanut Butter together and stir both of them evenly, until the yogurt is changing to a hue of brown
  4. Start pouring the mixture of milk and protein powder into the blender, do this as the last step so there isn't any of the mixture stuck at the bottom of it

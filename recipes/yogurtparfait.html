Yogurt Parfait Recipe

Ingredients

  • One and a Half cups of Aldi nonfat Greek Yogurt
  • Two Scoops of Peanut Butter
  • A cup of Protein Granola
  • One cup of strawberries
  • Honey
  1. Get a bowl and scoop the greek yogurt into it and don't stop until scale says 345 grams
  2. Get a Tablespoon and add 2 spoonfuls of Peanut Butter
  3. Once peanutbutter is added start mixing the Peanutbutter with the greek yogurt
  4. Next add a cup of protein granola to bowl and distribute it evenly
  5. Lastly, Add the strawberries to the cup and top it off with some honey and enjoy!

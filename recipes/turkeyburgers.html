Turkey Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Milk Bread
  • Citrus Garlic seasoning
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Chilli Powder
  • Cinnamon
  • Ground Turkey
  • Salad Mix

Steps For Making the Turkey Burger

  1. Get six ounces of ground turkey and roll it into a circular shape
  2. Begin to rub the spices into the patty at an even amount, keep rerubbing the patty and doing that by rerolling the turkey patty until the seasoining is even on both sides
  3. Cook Turkey Burger on medium heat and flip the burger once the color is brown
  4. Plate the burger onto a medium plate and score the avocado; add the avocado slices onto the milk bread
  5. Add salad mix and start shredding it into smaller pieces with knife dice the mix, then add it to the burger
  6. ENJOY!!
